Color Usage Guidelines

  • Racing Red: Primary CTAs, active navigation, brand elements, critical actions
  • Track Blue: Links, secondary buttons, information panels, help text
  • Pit Orange: Promotions, discounts, alerts, time-sensitive information
  • Grid Green: Success states, session go, confirmations, positive feedback
  • Caution Yellow: Warnings, pending states, important notices, caution periods
